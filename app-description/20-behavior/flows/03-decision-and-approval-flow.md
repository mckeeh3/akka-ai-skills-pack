# Flow: Decision and Approval

1. A workflow, policy gate, or agent creates a decision card.
2. Decision card includes evidence, risk, confidence, impact, policy trigger, and alternatives.
3. Authorized reviewer opens the card from the decision queue.
4. Reviewer approves, rejects, requests changes, escalates, or converts feedback into a policy proposal.
5. Workflow resumes, changes course, or remains blocked based on decision result.
6. Decision trace records reviewer, action, rationale, and linked policy/evidence.
