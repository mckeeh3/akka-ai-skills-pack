# Sprint 05: Audit, Trace, Governance, and Closed Loop

## Goal

Make Audit/Trace and Governance/Policy first-class core app capabilities rather than documentation-only expectations.

## Outcomes

- Audit/work trace capabilities, views, redaction, timeline surfaces, and access controls are complete enough for generation.
- Governance proposals, evaluation findings, approval/rejection, activation, rollback, and authority-expansion denial are connected to Agent Admin and workstream UI.
- Tests verify trace correlation and governance safety boundaries.
