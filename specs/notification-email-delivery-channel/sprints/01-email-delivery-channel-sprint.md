# Sprint 01: Notification Email Delivery Channel

## Objective

Implement or validate a bounded governed email delivery channel for notifications using Resend.

## Acceptance criteria

- Contract requires Resend for production email.
- Captured outbox supports local/dev/test validation.
- Preferences/category allowlist/redaction are enforced.
- My Account preference surface exists.
- Scaffolded backend/frontend checks pass.
