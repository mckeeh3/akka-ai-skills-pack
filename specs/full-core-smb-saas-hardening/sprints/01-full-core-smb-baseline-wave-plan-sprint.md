# Sprint 01: Full-Core SMB Baseline and Wave Plan

## Objective

Define the full-core SMB baseline and create an executable wave plan for child mini-projects.

## Scope

- SMB functional completeness definition.
- Five workstream capability/surface outline.
- Visual/UX quality bar for AI-first dashboards and structured surfaces.
- Agent-type selection rules for request/response agents, AutonomousAgent/internal worker agents, and deterministic services.
- Child mini-project sequence and first wave scaffolds.

## Acceptance criteria

- The baseline is specific enough to prevent scope drift into enterprise features or page-first CRUD.
- The wave plan supports one mini-project/sprint at a time.
- The first child mini-projects can begin without guessing product architecture.
- Runtime, provider, authorization, trace, and validation standards remain explicit.
