# Sprint 03: Workers, Digest/Export, and Governance Simulation

## Objective

Extend background task visibility, digest/export behavior, and policy simulation beyond the currently bounded implemented worker verticals.

## Scope

- Broader AutonomousAgent task notification coverage.
- Digest/export platform extensions beyond bounded personal/audit summaries.
- Policy simulation platform beyond Governance/Policy impact analysis.

## Acceptance criteria

- New worker/task behavior uses governed capabilities, `AutonomousAgent` runtime paths where model-driven, provider fail-closed behavior, v3 events, attention, result/progress surfaces, and tests.
- Digest/export operations preserve authorization, redaction, idempotency, and audit.
- Policy simulations cannot activate or expand authority without retained human approval.
