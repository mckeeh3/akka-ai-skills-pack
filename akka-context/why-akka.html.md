# Why Akka

Akka is the agentic systems platform built for Reliability, Risk Control, and Repeatability at enterprise scale.

## <a href="about:blank#_what_stands_between_you_and_reliable_ai"></a> What Stands Between You and Reliable AI

Three barriers prevent AI prototypes from becoming production-grade agentic systems.

### <a href="about:blank#_the_production_gap"></a> The Production Gap

AI frameworks built for demos cannot deliver the durability, resilience, and reliability that production systems demand. When your agent must process millions of transactions, survive hardware failures, and guarantee data consistency, most frameworks fail.

### <a href="about:blank#_liability"></a> Liability

Failures in resilience, governance, and safety carry severe legal, financial, and reputational consequences. Without a runtime that enforces policies, explains its decisions, and contains failures as they happen, agentic AI remains too risky to deploy at enterprise scale.

### <a href="about:blank#_the_specialist_trap"></a> The Specialist Trap

Consistent delivery breaks down when agentic AI depends on a handful of experts. One departure and your roadmap stalls. You need a platform that enables every team to deliver reliably, regardless of distributed systems expertise.

## <a href="about:blank#_how_akka_makes_ai_reliable"></a> How Akka Makes AI Reliable

Akka delivers reliable AI across three dimensions.

### <a href="about:blank#_reliability"></a> Reliability

Guaranteed, production-proven outcomes. Akka guarantees 99.9999% availability with active-active HA/DR and resilience backed by indemnities — contractual commitments, not aspirational targets. Sub-1 minute RTO, zero byte RPO. Proven over 18 years in more than 100,000 production deployments.

### <a href="about:blank#_risk_control"></a> Risk Control

Resilience, compliance, and sovereignty built in. Runtime-embedded governance enforces policies and contains failures as they happen — compliance built in, not bolted on. Self-explanation and self-containment are properties of every deployment, meeting the requirements of the EU AI Act and other emerging AI regulations.

### <a href="about:blank#_repeatability"></a> Repeatability

Consistent, scalable development across teams. One platform with golden-path workflows that give every team — regardless of role, skill set, or location — a consistent, governed path to production. Spec-Driven Development means anyone who can write requirements can build agentic systems.
