## Readiness rule

A row may move beyond `surface-ready` only when the missing runtime evidence is checked into specs/tests or recorded as validation output. Do not promote a workstream to `runtime-ready` from fixture rendering, frontend-only badges, deterministic provider bypasses, or service-only model calls outside the governed Akka Agent path.
